We've compiled answers to the most common questions from our research community. Can't find what you need? Our scientific advisory team responds within one business hour.

How does it ship and how do I store it? +
Our GLP-3R is supplied as a lyophilized (freeze-dried) powder in sealed vials — this format keeps the peptide stable and protected during shipping and storage. When it arrives, store the dry powder in a cool, dry, dark place. For longer storage, keep it at -20°C. Once you reconstitute it with bacteriostatic water or appropriate buffer, keep it in the fridge at 2–8°C and use it within 28–30 days. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles to preserve compound integrity.
How is it different from GLP-1S compounds? +
GLP-1S only activates one receptor (GLP-1). That's good, but it's like a one-trick pony. GLP-3R activates three receptors at once — GLP-1, GIP, and glucagon. The glucagon part is what really sets it apart, because it directly tells fat cells to burn up, which GLP-1S can't do at all.
What's in the Certificate of Analysis? +
The CoA is a document that proves the quality of what you're getting. It includes: the exact purity percentage from HPLC testing, mass spectrometry confirmation of the right molecule, batch number, date it was made, how to store it, and the molecular weight and formula. You can use it in your research documentation.
How pure is it? How do you check? +
Every batch is made to ≥99% chemical purity. We verify this using HPLC (a machine that separates and measures each chemical) and Mass Spectrometry (which confirms the exact molecule). A Certificate of Analysis comes with every order so you can see the numbers yourself. We also encourage you to run your own test.
Why is it supplied as a lyophilized powder? +
Lyophilized (freeze-dried) powder is the gold standard format for research peptides. Removing the water from the compound during manufacturing makes it much more stable — it can be stored at room temperature short-term or at -20°C long-term without degrading. This format also makes shipping easier and safer. When you're ready to use it in your research, simply reconstitute with sterile bacteriostatic water or an appropriate buffer solution. The result is a consistent, clean, measurable concentration every time — which is critical for reproducible research results.
What if I'm not happy with my order? +
Easy — just contact us within 30 days. If the product doesn't match the CoA, fails your own purity test, or arrives damaged, we'll send a replacement or give you a full refund. No questions asked. We want every researcher to be completely confident ordering from us.
